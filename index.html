02 · OPTIONS
How to combine the streams
Pick the merge mode. Replace drops the original audio; Mix blends both with the amix filter; Keep both writes them as two separate audio tracks (use MKV for that).
Stream-copy = seconds. Re-encode = minutes.
Positive = audio starts later. Negative = earlier.
Off = pad to longest stream.
